We strive to change the way software systems work, by adding gamification into the mix. Working and playing are like a machine. In which work is the engine and playing is the fuel for the engine. This working and playing relationship is of most important to achieve the perfect balance in enjoyment and efficiency whilst getting the job done. Gamification can help improve efficency in your workplace by helping to create enthusiasm and reducing stress for your employees.
